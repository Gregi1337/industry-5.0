What is INDUSTRY 5.0?

INDUSTRY 5.0 is one of the main existing applicable sustainable development business in combination with the upcycling-related industries, launched by Michael Rada in 2015. As of 2021 with more than 50+ ambassadors spread across each country.

The aim of Industry 5.0 is to build up a global network targeting the prevention of industrial waste to happen, so as eliminate the permanently increasing number of unused industrial so as residential sites and the number of unemployed worldwide. An industry that does not speak about sustainability but producing sustainability on a global level.

Existing INDUSTRY 5.0 waste categories being prevented are; PHYSICAL WASTE, SOCIAL WASTE, URBAN WASTE and PROCESS WASTE. Industry 5.0 applies the principles of LOGISTICS EFFICIENCY DESIGN (L.E.D.), these are:



TRANSPARENCY for all and everything.
PROFIT SHARING between all contributors and value-adding members.
EFFICIENCY in a global complex chain.

INDUSTRY 5.0 is here to change the world of business so as to reduce the negative impact of global industries and supply chains on our environment. Industry 5.0 AMBASSADORS are HUMANS that represents the INDUSTRY 5.0 principles on a national level in a globally harmonized action. Associated with INDUSTRY 5.0 AMBASSADORS NETWORK, which can be also found on LinkedIn.
